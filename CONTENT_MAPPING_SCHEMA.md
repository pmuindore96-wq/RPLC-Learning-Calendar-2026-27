# Content Mapping Schema: 7 Regenerative Agriculture Topics × 12-Month Calendar

## Overview
This document provides a detailed mapping of how the 7 core regenerative agriculture subtopics are distributed across a 12-month agricultural calendar, aligned with Indian farming seasons (Rabi, Kharif, and Zaid).

---

## Agricultural Seasons Context

| Season | Months | Crops | Climate |
|--------|--------|-------|---------|
| **Rabi** | Oct - Mar | Wheat, Chickpea, Mustard, Potato | Cool & Dry |
| **Kharif** | Jun - Sep | Rice, Cotton, Sugarcane, Maize, Pulses | Monsoon & Warm |
| **Zaid** | Apr - May | Summer vegetables, pulses, oilseeds | Hot & Dry |

---

## 7 Core Regenerative Agriculture Topics

### 1. 🌱 **Soil Health**
- Organic matter buildup
- Microbial community enhancement
- pH & nutrient balance
- Erosion control & compaction prevention
- Composting & mulching practices

### 2. 💨 **GHG Emission Reduction**
- Methane minimization (alternate wetting/drying)
- Carbon sequestration (agroforestry)
- Reduced nitrogen runoff
- Efficient energy use
- Livestock integration strategies

### 3. 💰 **Living Income**
- Diversified income streams
- Value-added products
- Market linkages & fair pricing
- Cost reduction through organic inputs
- Premium product certification

### 4. 💧 **Water Management**
- Rainwater harvesting
- Drip/micro-irrigation
- Groundwater conservation
- Watershed management
- Water quality monitoring

### 5. 🦋 **Biodiversity Preservation**
- Crop diversity & heirloom varieties
- Pollinator protection (native bees)
- Buffer zone creation
- Beneficial insect habitats
- Genetic resource documentation

### 6. 👩‍🌾 **Gender Equity**
- Women farmer decision-making
- Skill training & certification
- Leadership development
- Income equality & asset ownership
- Women-led producer groups

### 7. 🐄 **Animal Welfare**
- Integrated crop-livestock systems
- Humane livestock practices
- Animal nutrition & health
- Manure management
- Pasture integration

## Month-by-Month Breakdown

### **JANUARY - Rabi Season (Soil Prep & Planning)**
**Theme:** Foundation Building
**Typical Crops:** Wheat, Chickpea (Rabi crops in early stage)

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Soil Health** | Testing & Assessment | Soil sample collection, nutrient analysis, pH testing, organic matter evaluation |
| **Water Management** | Infrastructure Planning | Design rainwater harvesting, check groundwater levels, repair irrigation channels |
| **Biodiversity** | Baseline Mapping | Document existing field biodiversity, identify buffer zone locations |
| **Living Income** | Planning & Analysis | Analyze previous season's profitability, plan crop rotation for income |
| **GHG Reduction** | Baseline Assessment | Calculate baseline emissions from previous practices |
| **Gender Equity** | Needs Assessment | Identify women farmer participation in planning |
| **Animal Welfare** | System Design | Plan livestock integration if applicable |

**Key Training:** Soil Health Assessment, Organic Matter Management
**Field Tasks:** Soil testing, compost preparation, field boundary marking

---

### **FEBRUARY - Rabi Season (Sowing & Irrigation)**
**Theme:** Establishment Phase
**Typical Crops:** Wheat, Mustard, Potato (actively growing)

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Soil Health** | Amendment Application | Apply compost, bio-fertilizers, green manures |
| **Water Management** | Irrigation Efficiency | Install drip systems, mulch application, monitor moisture |
| **Seed Selection** | Quality & Genetics | Select certified organic seeds, store properly |
| **Living Income** | Input Cost Reduction | Use farm-made inputs, reduce expensive chemicals |
| **Biodiversity** | Variety Selection | Choose multiple crop varieties for diversity |
| **Gender Equity** | Decision-Making | Include women in seed/variety selection |
| **Animal Welfare** | Preparation | Integrate livestock for field preparation (if applicable) |

**Key Training:** Seed Selection & Treatment, Irrigation Efficiency
**Field Tasks:** Sowing, mulching, irrigation setup, bio-pesticide preparation

---

### **MARCH - Rabi Season (Growth & Nutrient Management)**
**Theme:** Vegetative Growth
**Typical Crops:** Wheat at tillering, Chickpea flowering begins

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Soil Health** | Nutrient Cycling | Apply vermicompost, monitor microbial activity, cover crops |
| **Water Management** | Conservation | Reduce irrigation frequency, monitor rainfall |
| **GHG Reduction** | Baseline Monitoring | Track emissions from current practices |
| **Pest Management** | IPM Implementation | Scout for pests, use bio-controls, trap crops |
| **Biodiversity** | Enhancement | Establish pollinator habitats, beneficial insect strips |
| **Gender Equity** | Capacity Building | Women farmers train in pest identification |
| **Animal Welfare** | Integration | Grazing management if livestock present |

**Key Training:** Nutrient Management in Rabi, Pest & Disease Management
**Field Tasks:** Bio-fertilizer application, field scouting, weed management

---

### **APRIL - Rabi Season (Late Growth & Preparation)**
**Theme:** Pre-Harvest Preparation
**Typical Crops:** Wheat heading, Chickpea pod filling

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Soil Health** | Pre-Harvest Assessment | Monitor soil condition, assess compaction |
| **Water Management** | Stress Management | Final irrigation scheduling, water conservation |
| **Living Income** | Harvest Planning | Plan marketing, identify value-addition opportunities |
| **Biodiversity** | Final Monitoring | Document pest-predator ratios, pollination success |
| **Gender Equity** | Labor Planning | Ensure women involved in harvest planning |
| **GHG Reduction** | Monitoring | Record emissions data from practices |
| **Animal Welfare** | Residue Utilization | Plan straw management for livestock feed |

**Key Training:** Water Stress Management, Harvest Readiness
**Field Tasks:** Harvest preparation, equipment maintenance, storage planning

---

### **MAY - Zaid Season (Summer Crops & Monsoon Prep)**
**Theme:** Transition & Water Security
**Typical Crops:** Summer vegetables, melons, pulses

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Water Management** | Harvesting & Storage | Rainwater harvesting structure construction/repair |
| **Soil Health** | Residue Management | Incorporate rabi residues, green manuring |
| **Living Income** | Value Addition | Preserve harvest, create value-added products |
| **Biodiversity** | Conservation | Protect native seed stocks, create seed banks |
| **Gender Equity** | Income Control | Women control profits from value-added products |
| **Pest Management** | Preparation | Prepare bio-pesticide stocks for monsoon |
| **Animal Welfare** | Feed Planning | Plan livestock feed from summer crops |

**Key Training:** Summer Crop Management, Water Harvesting Systems
**Field Tasks:** Water harvesting construction, residue incorporation, nursery setup

---

### **JUNE - Kharif Season (Monsoon Prep & Sowing)**
**Theme:** Monsoon Establishment
**Typical Crops:** Rice, Cotton, Maize (sowing/early growth)

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Water Management** | Monsoon Systems | Establish drainage, bunds, water control structures |
| **Soil Health** | Waterlogging Prevention | Build raised beds, improve drainage, add organic matter |
| **GHG Reduction** | Practice Setup | Establish alternate wetting/drying (AWD) for rice |
| **Biodiversity** | Habitat Creation | Create field margins, buffer strips, fish ponds |
| **Living Income** | Kharif Planning | Select high-value crops, plan intercropping |
| **Gender Equity** | Leadership | Women lead field preparation activities |
| **Animal Welfare** | Integration | Plan crop-livestock integration systems |

**Key Training:** Kharif Crop Planning, Soil & Water Conservation
**Field Tasks:** Bund construction, nursery management, sowing, water management setup

---

### **JULY - Kharif Season (Active Growth)**
**Theme:** Nutrition & Biodiversity
**Typical Crops:** Rice tillering, Cotton flowering, Maize growth

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Soil Health** | Nutrient Cycling | Organic manure application, mulching, microbial enhancement |
| **Biodiversity** | Active Management | Monitor pollinators, maintain buffer zones, beneficial insect habitats |

| **Water Management** | Flood Management | Monitor rainfall, manage excess water, maintain bund integrity |
| **Gender Equity** | Skill Training | Women train in pest identification, soil management |
| **Animal Welfare** | Fodder Production | Produce fodder for livestock, manage grazing |

**Key Training:** Nutrient Cycling in Kharif, Biodiversity Enhancement
**Field Tasks:** Nutrient application, crop monitoring, pest scouting, weed management

---

### **AUGUST - Kharif Season (Peak Growth & GHG Focus)**
**Theme:** Greenhouse Gas Mitigation
**Typical Crops:** Rice/Cotton in peak vegetative phase

| Topic | Focus Area | Activities |
|-------|-----------|-----------|

| **Water Management** | Irrigation Timing | Monitor rainfall, adjust irrigation for GHG reduction |
| **Gender Equity** | Decision Making | Women participate in resource allocation decisions |

**Key Training:** Greenhouse Gas Reduction, Animal-Crop Integration
**Field Tasks:** Water management, livestock integration, pest monitoring, nutrient tracking

---

| **Animal Welfare** | Feed Planning | Prepare straw and residues for livestock winter feed |

**Field Tasks:** Harvest logistics, team organization, storage preparation, residue planning
